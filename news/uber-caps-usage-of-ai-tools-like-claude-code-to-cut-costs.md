description: "Uber Technologies has introduced monthly usage caps on AI-powered coding tools for employees after exhausting its annual AI budget earlier this year. The company has capped spending at $1,500 per month per AI coding tool, including platforms such as Cursor and Claude Code, while allowing employees to seek approvals for higher usage. Uber said the move is aimed at managing costs while continuing AI adoption across teams."
